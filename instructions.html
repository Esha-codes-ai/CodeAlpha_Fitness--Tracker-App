How to Use the Me

  1. Login with username, age, email & password.
  2. After submit → you go to Home page.
  3. Check fitness images for motivation.
  4. Go to Workout page.
  5. Add workout name and duration.
  6. Total workout minutes will be calculated automatically.
  7. If you forget password → click Forgot Password.
  8. Reset password using your email.
